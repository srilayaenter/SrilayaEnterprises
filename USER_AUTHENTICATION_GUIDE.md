- Add items to your cart

- Click "Proceed to Checkout" in your cart

1. Click "Create New Account"
2. Fill in your details:
   - **Full Name**: Your name for delivery

- You'll be automatically taken to the checkout page
- Your cart items are preserved
- Fill in delivery details and complete payment

- Your saved addresses and details are available
- Complete payment quickly

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to login to browse products?
No, you can browse all products without logging in. Login is only required when you want to complete a purchase.

### Can I checkout as a guest?
Currently, we require account creation for all orders to ensure proper order tracking and customer service. Creating an account takes less than a minute!

### What if I don't want to create an account right now?
You can click "Continue Shopping" in the authentication dialog and keep browsing. Your cart items will be saved, and you can checkout later when you're ready.

### Will my cart items be lost if I login or register?
No! Your cart items are preserved throughout the login/registration process. After you login or create an account, you'll be taken directly to checkout with all your items intact.

### Can I use the same email for multiple accounts?
No, each email address can only be associated with one account. If you already have an account with an email, please use the login option instead.

### How do I know if I already have an account?
If you've ordered from us before or created an account, you already have one. Try logging in with your email. If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" option.

### Is my payment information stored?
We use secure payment gateways and do not store your complete payment information. You'll need to enter payment details for each order.

### Can I change my email address?
Yes, you can update your email address in your account settings after logging in.

### What happens if I forget my password?
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. We'll send you an email with instructions to reset your password.

### Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can request account deletion by contacting our customer support. Please note that this will remove all your order history and loyalty points.
