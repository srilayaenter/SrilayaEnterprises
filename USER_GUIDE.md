## Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Click **Register** in the top navigation
2. Fill in your details:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Phone number
   - Full name
3. Click **Create Account**
4. You'll be automatically logged in

### Logging In
1. Click **Login** in the top navigation
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

## Customer Features

### 1. Browsing Products

#### Home Page
- View all available organic products
- Use the **search bar** to find specific products
- Filter by category using the category buttons:
  - Millets, Rice, Flour, Flakes, Sugar, Honey, Laddus

#### Product Cards
- Each product shows:
  - Product image
  - Name and description
  - Base price
  - Category badge
  - **Heart icon** (wishlist button) - appears when logged in

### 2. Product Details

#### Viewing Product Details
1. Click on any product card or **View Details** button
2. Product detail page shows:
   - Large product image
   - Full description
   - Available packaging sizes with prices
   - Discount badges (if applicable)
   - Wishlist button (heart icon)

#### Adding to Cart
1. Select your preferred **packaging size**
2. Adjust **quantity** using + and - buttons
3. Click **Add to Cart**
4. A confirmation toast will appear

### 3. Wishlist Feature

#### Adding to Wishlist
- Click the **heart icon** on any product card or detail page
- The heart turns red when added to wishlist
- Click again to remove from wishlist

#### Managing Wishlist
1. Click the **heart icon** in the header (shows count)
2. View all your saved products
3. Click **View Details** to see product information
4. Click **Remove** to delete from wishlist
5. Use **Clear All** to empty your entire wishlist

### 4. Shopping Cart

#### Viewing Cart
- Click the **shopping cart icon** in the header
- Badge shows total number of items

#### Managing Cart Items
- Adjust quantities with + and - buttons
- Click **Remove** to delete items
- View subtotal, discount, and total
- Click **Proceed to Checkout** when ready

### 5. Checkout Process

#### Shipping Information
1. Fill in delivery address:
   - Full name
   - Phone number
   - Complete address
   - City, State, PIN code
2. Click **Continue to Payment**

#### Payment
1. Select payment method:
   - **Credit/Debit Card**
   - **UPI**
   - **Net Banking**
   - **Cash on Delivery**
2. Click **Place Order**
3. You'll be redirected to payment success page

#### Redeeming Loyalty Points
- On checkout page, view your available points
- Enter points to redeem (minimum 100 points)
- Maximum 50% of order value can be paid with points
- 1 point = â‚¹1 discount
- Click **Apply Points** to use them

### 6. Order Tracking

#### Viewing Orders
1. Click **Orders** in the header
2. See all your orders with:
   - Order number and date
   - Total amount
   - Status (pending, confirmed, shipped, delivered, cancelled)
   - Items ordered

#### Order Details
- Click on any order to see:
  - Detailed item list
  - Shipping address
  - Payment information
  - Order timeline

### 7. Loyalty Points Program

#### Earning Points
- Earn points with every purchase
- Points are automatically added after order completion
- Check your points balance in the header

#### Viewing Points
1. Click on your **points balance** in the header
2. See three key metrics:
   - **Total Points**: Your current balance
   - **Discount Value**: Equivalent rupee value
   - **Expiring Soon**: Points expiring in next 30 days

#### Points History
- View all point transactions:
  - **Earned**: Points from purchases
  - **Redeemed**: Points used for discounts
  - **Expired**: Points that expired
  - **Bonus**: Special promotional points
- Filter by transaction type
- See date and description for each transaction

#### Redeeming Points
- Points can be redeemed during checkout
- Minimum redemption: 100 points
- Maximum: 50% of order value
- Points expire after 365 days

### 8. Product Reviews

#### Writing a Review
1. Go to any product detail page
2. Scroll to **Customer Reviews** section
3. Click **Write a Review** button
4. Select star rating (1-5 stars)
5. Write your review comment
6. Click **Submit Review**

#### Viewing Reviews
- See all reviews on product detail page
- Reviews show:
  - Star rating
  - Reviewer name
  - Date posted
  - Review comment
  - Verified purchase badge (if applicable)

#### Filtering Reviews
- **All Reviews**: See everything
- **5 Stars** to **1 Star**: Filter by rating
- **Verified Purchases**: Only reviews from confirmed buyers

#### Sorting Reviews
- **Most Recent**: Newest reviews first
- **Highest Rated**: 5-star reviews first
- **Lowest Rated**: 1-star reviews first

### 9. Notifications

#### Notification Bell
- Click the **bell icon** in the header
- Red badge shows unread notification count
- Dropdown shows recent notifications

#### Notification Types
- **Order Updates**: Status changes, shipping updates
- **Loyalty Points**: Points earned, expiring soon
- **Promotions**: Special offers and discounts
- **System**: Important account updates

#### Managing Notifications
1. Click **View All** in notification dropdown
2. See all notifications with:
   - Type indicator (colored dot)
   - Title and message
   - Timestamp
3. Click **Mark as Read** on individual notifications
4. Use **Mark All as Read** to clear all unread
5. Click **Clear All** to delete all notifications

### 10. Customer Support Chat

#### Starting a Chat
1. Click the **chat bubble icon** (bottom-right corner)
2. Chat widget appears on all pages
3. Badge shows unread message count

#### Using Chat
1. Type your message in the input box
2. Press **Enter** or click **Send**
3. Messages appear in real-time
4. Admin responses show with "Admin" label
5. Your messages show with "You" label

#### Chat Features
- Real-time messaging
- Message timestamps
- Unread message counter
- Minimize/maximize chat window
- Available on all pages

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't Add to Cart
- **Solution**: Make sure you're logged in and have selected a packaging size

#### Wishlist Button Not Showing
- **Solution**: You must be logged in to use the wishlist feature

#### Points Not Applying at Checkout
- **Check**: 
  - Minimum 100 points required
  - Maximum 50% of order value
  - Points must not be expired

#### Reviews Not Submitting
- **Check**:
  - You must be logged in
  - Star rating is required
  - Comment cannot be empty

#### Chat Messages Not Sending
- **Check**:
  - You must be logged in
  - Message cannot be empty
  - Check internet connection

#### Notifications Not Updating
- **Solution**: Refresh the page or click the notification bell again

### Getting Help

#### Customer Support
- Use the **chat widget** (bottom-right corner)
- Send a message to admin
- Response typically within business hours

#### Contact Information
- Email: support@srilayaenterprises.com
- Phone: [Your phone number]
- Business Hours: [Your hours]
